Washington State, USA

Washington state sits in the northwest corner of the United states on the Pacific ocean. It is know for its rainy weather, tech companies like Microsoft and Amazon, and also brands like Starbucks and Costco. Washington is split in half by the cascade mountains leaving the east side of the state warmer and dryer then the west. Washington has a lot of natural beauty and is a great place to spend time outdoors in the warm summer months going on hikes or jumping into the frigid puget sound.
